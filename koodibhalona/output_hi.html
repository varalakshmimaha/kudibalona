About कूडीबालोण Trust

Empowering Communities, Changing Lives.

कूडीबालोण Trust is a public charitable organization based in Bangalore, Karnataka, founded with a vision to serve humanity beyond the boundaries of caste, creed, gender, or religion.

Established by Mr. Sai Jay Shankar B.C., the Trust is committed to creating a compassionate and inclusive society through impactful initiatives that touch every aspect of human life.
